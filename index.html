The Suite

Two tools. One mission.

Purpose-built Chrome extensions that integrate directly into Paragon.

STAR Tracker

v3.6

Real-time AHT tracking with a floating widget, multi-profile dashboards, auto-detection of queues, and dark mode for Paragon. Your cases, your pace.

  • ✓ Live AHT timer with color-coded thresholds
  • ✓ Floating AHT widget on every page
  • ✓ Paragon dark mode
  • ✓ Auto-accept cases
  • ✓ Auto-clean missed contacts
  • ✓ Auto-open attachments
  • ✓ Keyword alerts

STAR Annotations

v1.6

Smart annotation injection for Paragon cases. Categorize findings with a visual editor and one-click insertion into any case.

  • ✓ Category-based annotations (Grant, No Grant, Miscellaneous)
  • ✓ Visual rich-text editor with one-click insertion
  • ✓ Folder organization within each category for better structure
  • ✓ Autofill for Grant emails
  • ✓ Autofill for dates and tracking numbers
